About Me

Associate Professor

Computer Systems Group

International Institute of Information Technology, Hyderabad

Dr. Suresh Purini is an Associate Professor at the International Institute of Information Technology, Hyderabad (IIIT-Hyderabad), where he heads the Computer Systems Group. He received his Ph.D. in Computer Science and Electrical Engineering from the University of Maryland, Baltimore County, his M.E. in Computer Science and Automation from the Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore, and his B.Tech. in Computer Science and Engineering from S.V. University College of Engineering, Tirupati. His research interests span compilers, computer architecture, and parallel and distributed systems. His current research focuses on the design of domain-specific accelerators, particularly machine-learning accelerators, and agentic AI systems for hardware accelerator development targeting FPGAs and ASICs.

He is also a certified Yoga Trainer and a Heartfulness Meditation Trainer, and is actively engaged in promoting heart-centered education at the school, college, and community levels.
